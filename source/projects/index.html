City of Chattanooga Open Data Policy

We worked with members of the Mayor's staff and the community to draft an an open data policy on GitHub. The executive order was signed by Mayor Andy Berke on May 31, 2014, and directs the city government to take inventory of its valuable data and subsequently open it to the public.

All the data is published to the open data portal hosted by the Public Library. This mean developers can use government data, such as code violations or recreational center locations for example, to build applications for the community. It also means that developers can work more closely with the city to tackle government service challenges.
